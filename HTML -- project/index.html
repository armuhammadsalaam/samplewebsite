About

Puro Roble Boxing Club is a social networking service dedicated to arranging boxing sparring sessions in a respectable and technical learning environment. All sessions require the use of proper equipment (headgear, mouthpiece, minimum 16oz gloves, athletic shoes). Sparring sessions are also timed and referreed by a presiding individual according to Amateur Boxing Rules; i.e only three 3min rounds, standing 8 count, 3 knockdown rule. We also take into account experience level and intensity level to get you the best sparring session per your needs. Puro Roble maintains an active list of amateur fighters looking for sparring partners.
